Our Story

Welcome to Majd Coffee Shop, a haven for coffee aficionados and seekers of sublime gastronomic experiences. Our narrative is one of passion, commitment, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in the realm of specialty coffee.

Founded in 2023, Majd Coffee Shop emerged as a vision to transcend the ordinary, curating a space where every sip is a journey, and every visit is an escape into the nuanced world of coffee craftsmanship.

Meet the Team

Our team is a mosaic of dedicated individuals, each contributing a unique skill set and an unwavering commitment to our craft. From skilled baristas who transform beans into art to the warm faces that welcome you, every member of the Majd Coffee Shop family is an architect of your memorable experience.

We believe in the alchemy that happens when expertise, passion, and genuine hospitality converge. Allow us to introduce you to the artisans behind your favorite cup, the architects of our warm ambiance, and the guardians of the Majd Coffee Shop legacy.
